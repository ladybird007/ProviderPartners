About Us

At Provider Partners Connect Care (PPCC), our journey began with a vision from a physician - for physicians.

With an unwavering commitment to excellence, we are dedicated to providing exceptional, coordinated care to Medicare Fee-for-Service Beneficiaries in long-term care facilities.

We share your passion for delivering high-quality healthcare to patients.

We believe it is our duty to empower you with real-time data to ensure they receive the best care possible. Our mission is to join you in your dedication to caring for high-needs patients.

Together, let's change healthcare.

When we partner to improve care, we reduce unnecessary utilization and unlock Medicare dollars to help your Facility and Practice continue improving care for this vulnerable patient population. Together, we are pioneering a new era of high-quality care and transforming the lives of our patients. With you at the heart of it all, we're committed to making a difference and nurturing a healthier, brighter future for everyone we serve.
