Improve Your Primary Care with Real-Time Data

We are designed specifically for long-term residents of Skilled Nursing and Assisted Living Facilities and their Primary Care Providers who have a traditional Medicare benefit.

Read More

Provider Partners Connect Care is a Medicare Accountable Care Organization (ACO) participating in the Realizing Equity, Access, and Community Health (REACH) Model. What makes us unique is our innovative approach to managing the care of these beneficiaries through software that monitors the facility's Electronic Health Records (EHRs) in real-time to respond to changes in condition and provide interventions to avoid unnecessary utilization.
