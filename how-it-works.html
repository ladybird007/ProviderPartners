How It Works

Provider Partners Connect Care In Action

Deliver your primary care services your way.

Continue to conduct health risk assessments and create personalized care plans tailored to your patients' needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Stay in the loop with real-time alerts and virtual care coordinators.

Stay connected with real-time alerts and virtual RN care coordinators to identify patients who may be experiencing changes in their condition and may require clinical intervention. It's about keeping you informed and equipped to provide the right care at the right time.

Document and submit claims to Medicare as you normally would.

Medicare will extract the encounter information and send it over to Provider Partners Connect Care (PPCC) to pay the claim at the enhanced payment rate.

Achieve quality and utilization goals for potential rewards.

Based on your performance in improving quality and reducing unnecessary utilization, you will have the chance to receive greater reimbursement. It's a rewarding opportunity to be recognized for your hard work and dedication to high-needs patients in long-term care.

You won't have to worry about any extra quality reporting, such as MIPS.

You focus on what truly matters - providing exceptional care to your patients.
